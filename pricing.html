Pricing is a Negotiation with Time

Our process is a commitment to the entire cinematic journey, not a calculation of clock-time. We do not charge by the hour; we operate on a full-project commission model. Every price reflects the deep creative development, the technical crew required for narrative depth, and the meticulous post-production needed to ensure that every final deliverable—whether film or photograph—is a verified masterpiece. You are not buying a service; you are investing in a timeless visual legacy.

The Comission Fine Print

Q: What if my event eceeds the listed hours?
A: Additional coverage is priced at a rate of $175–$250 per hour, per crew member. This ensures that overage time maintains the same level of quality and focused production.
Q: Do you travel internationally?
A: Yes. All packages are subject to travel and accommodation stipends outside of our base cities.
Q: What is your payment schedule?
A: A 30% retainer is required to secure the date. The remainder is due 30 days prior to the production date.
Q: What format are the final films delivered in?
A: All films are delivered in high-bitrate 4K (UHD) resolution using a professional codec optimized for both online sharing and archival quality. We prioritize long-term fidelity for your visual legacy.
Q: Do I receive all the raw footage?
A: Raw footage delivery is standard for our Sovereign and Epicenter commission tiers. For the Essence tier, the complete, unedited archive is available as a professional add-on.
Q: Who owns the copyright to the final delivered film?
A: The Fifth Dimension retains the copyright and license to use the film for promotional and portfolio purposes. However, you receive a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use the final film for all your personal, non-commercial needs. Commercial licensing terms are negotiated separately.
Q: How much input do I have on the final edit?
A: We view this as a collaboration. Our contracts include a structured revision stage where you can provide feedback on the first draft. This ensures the final film aligns with your vision while maintaining our cinematic quality standards.
Q: Can I choose the music for my film?
A: We curate all music from premium, cinematic music licensing libraries to ensure the legality and emotional resonance of the final film. While we welcome genre and style input, the final track selection is handled by our creative team to ensure full licensing compliance.
Q: How long does the post-production process take?
A: Due to our commitment to detailed, custom editing and color grading, the final delivery timeframe is typically 12–16 weeks from the final production date. A short teaser or trailer is often delivered within the first 4 weeks.
